Future of Design About Me

Currently, I am a research scientist at Palo Alto Research Center(a part ofSRI International). My research interests intersect computational mechanics and structural optimization.
My research interests are:

  • Topology Optimization: Multifunctional mechanism design, soft robotics, multi-physics design optimization, dynamic response.
  • Materials by design: Metamaterial design, biomimetic structures, inverse design
  • Design methods: model-order reduction, adjoint-free methods, machine learning driven structural design, structural design under uncertainty.
  • Computational fabrication: 4D printing
